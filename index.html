About Us

AndyForty Global Software Solutions provides innovative IT solutions tailored for schools, businesses, and communities.

AndyForty Software Technology (aSt) is a global organisation. The company has its head office in Zimbabwe and international support from Slovak Republic (aSc Applies Software Consultancy) and United Kingdom (DataEase). It provides technical support in software development, data and information management for local and global businesses since 2007. The technical support extends to organisations from all sectors of the economy addressing key software development issues.

Our Vision

To provide affordable, reliable and scalable systems for the Zimbabwean Education, Financial and Industrial Markets and the extended global market.

Our Mission

To provide software that is relevant to the unique economic policies and legislation which applies to each country’s market.
